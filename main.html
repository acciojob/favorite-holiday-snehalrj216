Goa


Goa is one of the most popular holiday destinations in India, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, rich Portuguese heritage, and delicious seafood. It is a perfect place for relaxation and adventure.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Baga Beach – famous for its nightlife
  2. Calangute Beach – the largest beach in Goa
  3. Fort Aguada – historic Portuguese fort
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – breathtaking natural beauty
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus – UNESCO World Heritage site

Must-Try Foods

  • Goan Fish Curry – traditional spicy dish
  • Prawn Balchão – tangy and flavorful
  • Vindaloo – famous Goan curry
  • Bebinca – classic Goan dessert
  • Feni – local traditional drink
