Goa


Goa is one of the most popular holiday destinations in India, known for its beautiful beaches, nightlife, and delicious food.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Baga Beach – famous for nightlife
  2. Calangute Beach – largest beach
  3. Fort Aguada – historic fort
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – scenic beauty
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus – heritage site

Must-Try Foods

  • Fish Curry – traditional dish
  • Prawn Balchão – spicy taste
  • Vindaloo – famous curry
  • Bebinca – sweet dessert
  • Feni – local drink
